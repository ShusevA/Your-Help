Worldwide leader

Whole world trusts us. We are reliable fundraising platform. We help you mobilize supporters and grow your fundraising strategy across the globe with a multi-language & multi-currency platform

Simple setup

You can personalize and share in just a few minutes.

24/7 expert advice

Our best-in-class customer happiness agents will answer your questions, day or night. We offer a full range of solutions to help you build successful long-term relationships with your supporters

Mobile app

Together makes it simple to launch and manage your campaign on the go.

Social reach

Harness the power of social media to spread your story and get more support. Whether your organisation is already international or exploring the possibility of expanding, we make the process easy and efficient for you to rally supporters globally.

Fast and secure

Most organizers can withdraw funds immediately and our team works around the clock to protect against fraud. Our platform is able to process 135 currencies, 23 different languages, and multiple local & international payment methods. With our integrated conversion system, you’ll be able to provide a cross-currency experience for your supporters, where you set goals in one currency, and can receive donations in another.
